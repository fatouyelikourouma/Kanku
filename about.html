Changing Africa, the decentralized way

Everytime you make a purchase through Kanku, a portion of your payment will go to an African charity of your choice. And if you’re worried whether your money will get there or not, don’t worry. Using blockchain tracking technology, you can make sure your donation gets to where you need it to be.

Supporting Diaspora Creators

Our pieces are made by artists from the diaspora. NFTs allows artists to make a profit and take control of their artwork. By making a purchase on Kanku, you are directly supporting arists across the diaspora.
